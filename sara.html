About Artist

"Born as a girl, she must always be beautiful wherever she is, on her wedding day, when going to a party, walking on the street. Always working with creativity in makeup style, regularly updated with new trends. What are you waiting for without coming to me."

This makeup look is exactly for the team of girls who are glamorous and don't like red lipstick. To have a really fresh and radiant appearance, we can not only wear red lipstick, pink lipstick, but also choose lighter lipstick colors, such as orange. The key is to cleverly combine cheek and eye color to bring out the vitality of the face.

Natural, gentle, feminine makeup. Just use different shades of coral to complete this makeup look. This makeup with sparkling coral tone is very eye-catching, especially for medium-skinned girls. The highlight of this makeup style is the glossy background and sparkling eyes that smile. The eyeshadow step only needs to use 3 coral colors with 3 tones from light to dark to create the ultimate omber effect.

This style is very suitable for stylish ladies with a naturally beautiful face but no less charming. Natural transparent makeup style, using colors closest to your skin tone, gives you a smooth and gentle look that, although simple, can completely enhance the natural contours of your face. Natural transparent makeup limits all the sharpness in each line, very suitable for the office environment - where girls are required to be meticulous and moderate.
