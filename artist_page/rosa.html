" Every bride wants to look best on her wedding day, that is understandable as this is the most important day in a girl's life. I'm here to help you, with a variety of makeup looks you won't be disappointed with my abilities."

Although it is a classic makeup, she should still choose bright tones to make her face more youthful. The cat's eyes can be a bit long to make the eyes more attractive. Incorporating some accessories like pearl earrings is a classic style standard..

Natural makeup will help her save time and still look beautiful. You just need to apply a little foundation, apply lipstick, press light eyeshadow and can use thin or slightly dark eyeliner as you like. The lipstick can be painted in an ombre to add a natural look.

This makeup look is for the girl with personality, dark skin or want to break a little! With this color tone, it is still in favor of a soft, natural makeup style, promoting the harmonious lines on the face, then adding a little bit of wine red to create a highlight. The advantage of the wine red color is that it is warm but still radiant, and also makes the face somewhat rosy and radiant.
